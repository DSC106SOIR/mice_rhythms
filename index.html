Mice Rhythms

Explore temperature rhythms in female and male mice using two interactive modes: Granularity and Time.
In Granularity Mode, use the slider to adjust temporal resolution (Minute, Hour, 12-Hour, or Day) and filter by estrus cycle status. Hovering reveals activity-driven motion.
In Time Mode, drag the vertical slider to scrub through each minute of the experiment and observe per-mouse temperature changes over time, with circle size indicating activity.
